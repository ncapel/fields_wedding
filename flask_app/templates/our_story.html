Our Story

In the enchanting ambiance of Go Burrito, Anesa and Tyler found themselves drawn to the allure of karaoke night. Amidst shared connections and the lively atmosphere, they gravitated towards each other, two souls on the cusp of a romantic overture. Tyler, captivated by Anesa's beauty, endeavored to kindle a conversation, and as destiny would have it, Anesa willingly danced along the notes of his words. Their connection reached its zenith when Anesa, fueled by courage and a melody in her heart, took center stage to serenade Tyler with Tom Petty's "Last Dance With Mary Jane." The sweet strains of the song echoed the burgeoning affection between them. That magical night culminated in the exchange of numbers, an ephemeral connection that, unbeknownst to Anesa, would defy the odds.

In the weeks that followed, Anesa grappled with the undeniable pull towards Tyler. Despite her attempts to elude the inevitable, Tyler's unwavering determination gradually chipped away at her resistance.
